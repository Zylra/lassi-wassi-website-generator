

# Lassi Wassi — Modern Café Website

## Overview
A vibrant, scroll-animated website for Lassi Wassi, a trendy fast-food café chain in Pune. The design will be youthful, premium, and energetic with bold yellows, oranges, cream, and dark contrast tones.

## Design System
- **Colors**: Vibrant orange/yellow primary, warm cream backgrounds, dark charcoal for contrast
- **Typography**: Bold display headings + clean sans-serif body (Inter/Poppins)
- **Animations**: Scroll-triggered reveals, hover effects on cards, floating food elements, smooth transitions

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Sticky Navbar
- Logo + nav links (About, Menu, Locations, Franchise)
- Smooth scroll navigation, transparent → solid on scroll

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-screen with gradient/animated background
- Tagline: "Sip the Vibe. Bite the Flavor."
- CTA buttons: "View Menu" and "Find Nearest Outlet"
- Floating food illustration elements

### 3. About Section
- Brand story with scroll-triggered fade-in animations
- Highlight cards: Fresh Ingredients, Fusion Menu, Youth Vibe

### 4. Menu Section
- Filterable visual grid with 15 categories (Shawarma, Lassi, Burgers, Coffee, Thick Shakes, etc.)
- Animated cards with hover effects showing item image placeholder, name, description, and price
- Full menu data from the provided list organized by category

### 5. Locations Section
- Cards for all 5 Pune outlets with addresses
- Google Maps embed
- "Get Directions" button linking to Google Maps for each location

### 6. Franchise Section
- Bold CTA: "Own a Lassi Wassi Franchise"
- Short pitch about expansion opportunity
- "Apply for Franchise" button → links to external Google Form (placeholder URL)

### 7. Testimonials Section
- Curated customer reviews from the provided data (featuring positive reviews)
- Star ratings display
- Instagram-style social proof grid

### 8. Footer
- Quick links, locations list, social media icons, contact info

## Technical Features
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Scroll-triggered animations using Intersection Observer
- Smooth scroll behavior
- All content from the provided menu and review data
- No backend needed — franchise button links externally
